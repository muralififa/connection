Who We are!

We are a specialized team focused on Enterprise Database Governance, ensuring security, compliance, and operational excellence across complex database landscapes. Our mission is to simplify and standardize database management for large-scale enterprise environments.

With hands-on experience across a wide range of platforms—including SQL Server, Azure SQL, Oracle (including RDS), PostgreSQL (Aurora & RDS), MySQL (Aurora & RDS), Amazon DocumentDB, and Sybase—we provide complete visibility and control over thousands of database instances.

Our core capabilities :

Daily compliance monitoring for 13,000+ enterprise databases, Credential vaulting and rotation automation integrated with CyberArk, Data collection and asset tracking using Snow Inventory and ServiceNow, Seamless connection testing and instance validation for critical environments, Performance optimization and secure configuration enforcement
